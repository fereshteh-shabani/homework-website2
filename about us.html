our mission

Diamond clinic

Is a well established premium aesthetic clinic created to offer personalised, evidence-based scientific approaches for skin and body treatments in a modern, relaxed and familiar atmosphere in a great happening location in the heart of Notting Hill. Our expert team led by a passionate and energetic medical director, is committed to provide the very best aesthetic treatments for all skin conditions. With a combined experience of over 20 years in medicine, aesthetics and skincare, we strive to deliver results and offer the best customer experience.

Why Diamond clinic

We offer customised and premium services and products using the most recent scientific advances and research. We understand and encourage early prevention and treatment of skin changes hence our extremely highly-qualified team will make sure you are in the most knowledgeable hands. We feel that is essential to book an initial consultation to carefully examine and analyse your skin first. Based on our findings and assessment, we will develop a bespoke aesthetic skin care plan for you. This will include personalised recommendations for your daily skincare routine at home as well as the right type of facials and other non-surgical procedures which will benefit your skin. At our Clinic we are embracing and actively supporting the concept of early prevention and treatment of your whole complexion rather than treating isolating lines and wrinkles which will boost your inner beauty and confidence rather than create artificial results. We want you to feel amazing in your skin! We will enhance your confidence and nurture your happiness and healthy look inside and outside!
